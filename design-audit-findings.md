## DESIGN CONSISTENCY FINDINGS

### ✅ Positive Findings:
1. **Color Scheme**: Consistent NEON green (#c8ff00) and black theme across all pages
2. **Typography**: Consistent font usage throughout
3. **Button Styles**: Consistent neon-glow button styling
4. **Navigation**: Header present on all public pages
5. **Footer**: Present on all public pages with consistent links
6. **Breadcrumbs**: Present on internal pages
7. **Mobile Responsiveness**: Hamburger menu working on mobile
8. **Social Proof**: Real people photos now showing in bubbles

### ✅ Fixed Issues:
1. Title changed from "TIRED OF CRASHING?" to "FUEL YOUR POTENTIAL"
2. Cloned site title changed from "FUEL YOUR FUTURE" to "FUEL YOUR POTENTIAL"
3. Cartoon avatars replaced with real people photos
4. Hamburger menu now shows all 12 tabs
