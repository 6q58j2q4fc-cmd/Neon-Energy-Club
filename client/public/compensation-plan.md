## 7 WAYS TO EARN

### 1. RETAIL PROFITS (25-40%)
Earn immediate profits on every sale you make directly to customers.
- **Retail Markup:** 25-40% profit margin
- **No inventory required** - drop-ship directly to customers

### 2. FAST START BONUS ($50-$200)
Earn bonuses when you personally enroll new distributors.
- **Starter Kit:** $50 bonus
- **Builder Kit:** $100 bonus
- **Business Kit:** $200 bonus

### 3. TEAM COMMISSIONS (5-20%)
Earn commissions on your entire team's sales volume.

| Rank | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 | Level 4 | Level 5 |
|------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Bronze | 5% | 3% | - | - | - |
| Silver | 7% | 5% | 3% | - | - |
| Gold | 10% | 7% | 5% | 3% | - |
| Platinum | 12% | 10% | 7% | 5% | 3% |
| Diamond+ | 15% | 12% | 10% | 7% | 5% |

### 4. BINARY BONUS (10-15%)
Earn weekly bonuses based on your weaker leg's volume.
- **Bronze-Gold:** 10% of lesser leg volume (max $2,500/week)
- **Platinum:** 12% of lesser leg volume (max $5,000/week)
- **Diamond+:** 15% of lesser leg volume (max $25,000/week)

### 5. MATCHING BONUS (5-25%)
Earn a percentage of your personally enrolled distributors' binary bonuses.
- **1st Generation:** 25%
- **2nd Generation:** 10%
- **3rd Generation:** 5%

### 6. LEADERSHIP POOLS (2% of Company Volume)
Qualified leaders share in monthly bonus pools.
- **Diamond Pool:** 0.5% of company volume
- **Crown Diamond Pool:** 0.75% of company volume
- **Royal Diamond Pool:** 0.75% of company volume

### 7. RANK ADVANCEMENT BONUSES
One-time bonuses when you achieve new ranks.
