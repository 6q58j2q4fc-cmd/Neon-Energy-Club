# NEON Energy Drink Brand Content

## Brand Story
- Founded 2013 in Bend, Oregon, USA
- Founded by Harvard Business School Alumni Dakota Rea
- Mission: "Improving the drinking health of the next generation"
- Repurchased assets during COVID-19 pandemic for global relaunch
- Grew to 48 states in first year
- Expanded to 14 countries within 5 years
- Total gross sales reached $121,482,250 by July 2020
