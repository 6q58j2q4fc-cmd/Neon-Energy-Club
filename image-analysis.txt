Fruit Slider Image Analysis
============================

Images with transparent backgrounds (PNG with alpha):
- /cranberry.png - transparent background (checkered pattern visible)
- /strawberry.png - transparent background (checkered pattern visible)
- /raspberry.png - white/light background (NOT transparent)
- /images/blueberries.png - needs checking

Images with solid backgrounds (JPG - no transparency):
- /cherry.jpg - light gray/white background
- /gotu-kola.jpg - full green leaf photo (no transparency)
- /images/passion-fruit.jpg - needs checking
- /images/prickly-pear.jpg - needs checking
- /images/green-tea-leaves.jpg - needs checking

Issues identified:
1. raspberry.png appears to have a white background, not transparent
2. JPG files cannot have transparency - cherry.jpg, gotu-kola.jpg have solid backgrounds
3. Inconsistent mix of transparent PNGs and solid background JPGs

Solution approach:
- Since images are displayed in circular containers with overflow:hidden, the background inconsistency is visible
- Best fix: Add a dark background color to the image container to normalize appearance
- Alternative: Replace JPG images with transparent PNG versions
