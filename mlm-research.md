# MLM Compensation Plan Research

## Key Findings for NEON Energy Drink

### Recommended Structure: Hybrid (Binary + Unilevel)

Based on research of successful energy drink MLMs and industry best practices, a **hybrid compensation plan** combining binary and unilevel structures is optimal for NEON.

### Compensation Plan Structure

**1. Binary Team Commissions (Primary Income)**
- 10% commission on weaker leg volume
- Weekly payouts
- Max $10,000 per week cap
- Encourages teamwork and spillover

**2. Unilevel Fast Start Bonuses**
- Level 1 (Personal): 20%
- Level 2: 10%
- Level 3: 5%
- Level 4-5: 3%
- Rewards immediate recruiting

**3. Rank Advancement Bonuses**
- Associate: $0
- Manager: $500 bonus
- Director: $2,500 bonus
- Executive: $10,000 bonus
- Presidential: $25,000 bonus

**4. Matching Bonuses (Leadership)**
- 10% matching on Level 1 leaders
- 5% matching on Level 2-3 leaders
- Requires Executive rank or higher

### Package Structure

**Distributor Entry Packages:**
1. **Starter Pack** - $199
   - 12 cans NEON Original
   - 12 cans NEON Organic
   - Business kit & training
   - Qualifies for commissions

2. **Pro Pack** - $499
   - 48 cans mixed (Original + Organic)
   - Premium business kit
   - Marketing materials
   - Personal website
   - Higher commission tier

3. **Elite Pack** - $999
   - 144 cans mixed
   - Complete marketing suite
   - Premium training access
   - VIP support
   - Highest commission tier

**Customer Packages:**
1. **Single Purchase** - $3.99/can
2. **12-Pack** - $42 ($3.50/can)
3. **24-Pack** - $72 ($3/can)
4. **Auto-Ship Monthly** - 15% discount

### Commission Payout Balance

**Total Payout**: 40-45% of revenue
- Binary commissions: 20%
- Fast start bonuses: 10-15%
- Rank bonuses: 5%
- Matching bonuses: 5-10%
- Company profit: 55-60%

This structure balances:
- Distributor earnings potential
- Company profitability
- Product movement (not just recruiting)
- Sustainable growth

### Key Success Factors
1. Product-focused (70% retail, 30% recruiting)
2. Reasonable entry costs
3. No inventory loading
4. Clear rank advancement
5. Weekly payouts
6. Mobile-friendly tools
